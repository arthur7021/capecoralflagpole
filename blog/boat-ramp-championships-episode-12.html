Burrowing Al Reports · Episode 12

The Boat Ramp Championships

Published July 12, 2026

It's hour three at the Cape Coral boat ramp, and the line isn't moving. Burrowing Al is on the scene with a clipboard and a whistle, judging the day's competitors on backing a trailer in straight — a skill that, it turns out, is in very short supply. One driver jackknifes so badly the truck ends up facing the wrong lake. Another gets a round of applause just for staying inside the lines. Al scores every attempt like an Olympic judge while the 250-ft flagpole watches over the whole spectacle from the shore, unbothered as ever.
